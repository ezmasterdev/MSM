Frequently Asked Questions



How do I order my MSM favourites?

Simply open the app of our trusted partners and search for us inside their application. MSM should appear in the list right after you click search. From there, you can browse our menu and order your MSM favourites.

Are all the items available for delivery?

We've optimized our menu for delivery in order to uphold the high standards of quality and consistency that you have come to expect from us. As a result, some food and drink items aren't available for delivery right now.

How should I pay for my order?

Our trusted delivery partners offer a variety of payment methods for online food orders, such as PayPal, GCash, credit or debit card payments, and cash on delivery. Feel free to choose whichever method you prefer.

Is MSM delivery available near me?

To see if MSM delivery is available near your area, Open your Grabfood, foodpanda, or Pick.A.Roo app and enter your location. Check if the delivery services of our trusted partners are available in your area.

What if I encounter problems?

If you are experiencing problems with your delivery, please contact the GrabFood, foodpanda, or Pick.A.Roo customer support to properly assess the situation and provide you instructions for the necessary steps.
